Questions to ask friends

If you’re interviewing Bahá’í friends about what the site should cover, these prompts surface gaps the question bank may miss:

  • What’s a Bahá’í life decision you postponed because you weren’t sure what the guidance said?
  • What did your Assembly or community get asked that no article seemed to answer?
  • What do seekers or friends mistake for Bahá’í law but is actually culture or conscience?
  • What are you preparing for this year — a Holy Day, Ayyám-i-Há, a funeral, a wedding, a move?
  • What topic do Ruhi books touch on that you still find confusing in practice?
