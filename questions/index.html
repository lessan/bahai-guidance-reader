Questions

Browse all questions grouped by topic hub. Explore is the recommended starting point.

Community & the Plan

  • How are the goals of the Nine Year Plan being tracked, and what is the data discipline behind those numbers?
  • How do the three protagonists (individual, institutions, community) actually interact in a cluster — and what goes wrong when one of the three is weak?
  • How does a cluster move from the first to the second milestone, and what is the qualitative shift involved?
  • How does the Plan account for the diversity of national and cultural contexts — i.e., what is uniform across all clusters, and what is contextual?
  • What are the three dimensions of Bahá’í endeavour (community-building / social action / discourse), and how do they relate as one organic whole?
  • What does it actually mean to 'release the society-building power of the Faith' — what is being released, into what, and how is success measurable?
  • What does it mean for a cluster to enter the 'third milestone' — and why is that the threshold at which the *society-building power of the Faith* begins to be released?
  • What is a Regional Bahá’í Council, and how does it differ from a National Spiritual Assembly?
  • What is a learning site, and why is it called a 'site' rather than a 'centre'?
  • What is an Area Teaching Committee, who appoints it, and what is its specific function?
  • What is an Intensive Programme of Growth, what makes one intensive, and what are its constituent elements?
  • What is the cycle of activity, and why is the three-month cycle the operative tempo rather than a year, a month, or a week?
  • What is the difference between an advanced cluster and an emerging cluster — and how should each be approached?
  • What is the institute process, why is it called a 'process' rather than a 'course,' and what does its maturity look like?
  • What is the relationship between the Ruhi sequence of courses and the four core activities — i.e., which book builds capacity for which activity?
  • What is the role of the Auxiliary Board member and the Auxiliary Board assistant in advancing the Plan?
  • What is the role of the Local Spiritual Assembly in the cluster era — and how is that role evolving?
  • What is the role of the National Growth Committee that the Nine Year Plan introduced, and how does it relate to the Regional Bahá’í Council?

Compare & Clarify

  • How do The Promise of World Peace, The Prosperity of Humankind, and One Common Faith compare?
  • How do Tier A, Tier B, and Tier C authority differ, and why does Bahá’í epistemology require all three?
  • How does Bahá’í consultation differ from Quaker meeting, kibbutz consensus, or corporate deliberation?
  • How does Bahá’í social action differ from secular development and from missionary charity?
  • How does a neighbourhood differ from a village, and how do both relate to the cluster?
  • How does consolidation differ from deepening differ from capacity building?
  • How does teaching differ from direct teaching differ from meaningful conversation — and which is appropriate when?
  • How does the Bahá’í Covenant differ from the Old Testament covenant or the Christian new covenant?
  • How does the Bahá’í Faith's view of religion differ from perennialism or syncretism?
  • How does the Bahá’í concept of gender equality differ from secular feminism?
  • How does the Bahá’í institutional architecture differ from prior religious traditions?
  • How does the Counsellors' arm differ from Spiritual Assemblies — and what is meant by 'the learned and the rulers'?
  • How does the Nine Year Plan (2022–2031) differ from the Five Year Plan (2016–2021)?
  • How does the Ruhi Institute's pedagogical method differ from conventional adult education?
  • How does the Universal House of Justice differ from the Guardianship in nature, function, and authority?
  • How does the cluster era (post-2001) differ from the mass-teaching era (1964–1996)?
  • How does the society-building framing differ from 'entry by troops', and what continuities exist?

The Bahá'í Faith

  • What does 'infallibility' mean for the Universal House of Justice — and how does that differ from the infallibility of Bahá’u’lláh, the Báb, and ’Abdu’l-Bahá?
  • What does Bahá’u’lláh mean by 'the independent investigation of truth,' and how does that coexist with obedience to the institutions?
  • What does Bahá’u’lláh say about the soul, its nature, and its progress after death?
  • What is consultation as an *epistemology* (not just a procedure), and how does it differ from democratic voting, deliberation, or expert deliberation?
  • What is meant by 'the spiritual conquest of the planet,' and how is that compatible with the Bahá’í prohibition on partisan political involvement?
  • What is the Administrative Order, and how is it different from any other religious organisation in history?
  • What is the Covenant, and why is it called the 'axis' of the Faith?
  • What is the difference between the Bahá’í concept of 'society-building' and the secular development paradigm?
  • What is the difference between the Most Great Peace and the Lesser Peace, and where do we currently stand in that arc?
  • What is the institution of the Mashriqu'l-Adhkár, and what role does it play that no other institution can play?
  • What is the meaning of the Manifestation of God, and how does the doctrine of progressive revelation fit with the finality and uniqueness of each Manifestation?
  • What is the meaning of the equality of women and men in Bahá’í teaching, and how is the membership of the UHJ a clarification rather than a contradiction of that principle?
  • What is the relationship between the Greater Covenant (between God and humanity) and the Lesser Covenant (between Bahá’u’lláh and the believers)?
  • What is the spiritual meaning of work, and what does Bahá’u’lláh mean by 'work performed in the spirit of service is worship'?
  • What is the station of ’Abdu’l-Bahá — and what does 'the Centre of the Covenant' actually mean?
  • Why is the Guardianship — held only by Shoghi Effendi — not continued in his successors, and what does that mean for the authority of the UHJ today?
  • Why is the principle of the oneness of humanity the axial principle of Bahá’u’lláh’s revelation, and not just one principle among many?
